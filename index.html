Real Rice

Grow virtual rice on your mobile device, and turn it into real rice to feed people in need around the world!

Feed the world and make a difference while simply having fun growing virtual rice on your phone for free!

How it works

The revenue earned in "Real Rice" through advertising and in app purchases are used to match the rice donations made in the game. Donations are made to the World Food Program.
